Shark Pack is a community driven collection of 10k randomly generated ERC-721 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain. Our objective is to create the largest school of sharks in the metaverse. By joining the pack it grants you full rights to the shark artwork and a cool new PFP to flex among your fellow degens. We will be making an impact in the ocean by donating post mint to our chosen charity Shark Allies, to help safeguard the future of our sharks.
